   - Individual productivity tools don't enable team alignment
   - No shared knowledge repository for teams
   - Missing real-time collaboration on goals

#### Market Validation:
- 85% of professionals report struggling with productivity

- $1.4B productivity software market growing 12% YoY

- **Mitigation**:

**Likelihood**: Medium | **Impact**: Medium
- **Mitigation**:
  - Use efficient models (not GPT-4)

  - Target margin > 75%
